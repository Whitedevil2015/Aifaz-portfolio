Durood Sharif

Blessings Upon the Prophet ﷺ

1

Durood-e-Ibrahim

Recited in Daily Prayers (Salah)

اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّعَلٰٓی اٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ کَمَا صَلَّیْتَ عَلٰٓی اِبْرٰاهِیْمَ وَعَلٰٓی اٰلِ اِبْرٰاهِیْمَ اِنَّکَ حَمِیْدٌ مَّجِیْدٌ ۝ اَللّٰهُمَّ بَارِکْ عَلٰی مُحَمَّدٍ وَّعَلٰٓی اٰلِ مُحَمَّدٍ کَمَا بَارَکْتَ عَلٰٓی اِبْرٰاهِیْمَ وَعَلٰٓی اٰلِ اِبْرٰاهِیْمَ اِنَّکَ حَمِیْدٌ مَّجِیْدٌ

Allāhumma ṣalli 'alā Muḥammadin wa 'alā āli Muḥammad, kamā ṣallayta 'alā Ibrāhīma wa 'alā āli Ibrāhīm, innaka Ḥamīdum Majīd. Allāhumma bārik 'alā Muḥammadin wa 'alā āli Muḥammad, kamā bārakta 'alā Ibrāhīma wa 'alā āli Ibrāhīm, innaka Ḥamīdum Majīd

Translation: O Allah! Bestow Your blessings upon Muhammad and his family as You blessed Ibrahim and his family. Indeed, You are Praiseworthy and Glorious. O Allah! Bestow Your favours upon Muhammad and his family as You blessed Ibrahim and his family. Indeed, You are Praiseworthy and Glorious.

2

Durood-e-Tunajjina

For Relief from Difficulties & Protection

اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی سَیِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ صَلَاۃً تُنْجِیْنَا بِهَا مِنْ جَمِیْعِ الْاَهْوَالِ وَالْاٰفَاتِ وَتَقْضِیْ لَنَا بِهَا جَمِیْعَ الْحَاجَاتِ وَتُطَهِّرُنَا بِهَا مِنْ جَمِیْعِ السَّیِّاٰتِ وَتَرْفَعُنَا بِهَا عِنْدَکَ اَعْلَی الدَّرَجَاتِ وَتُبَلِّغُنَا بِهَا اَقْصَی الْغَایَاتِ مِنْ جَمِیْعِ الْخَیْرَاتِ فِی الْحَیٰوۃِ وَبَعْدَ الْمَمَاتِ

Allāhumma ṣalli 'alā sayyidinā Muḥammadin ṣalātan tunajjīnā bihā min jamī'il ahwāli wal āfāt, wa taqḍī lanā bihā jamī'al ḥājāt, wa tuṭahhirunā bihā min jamī'is sayyi'āt, wa tarfa'unā bihā 'indaka a'lad darajāt, wa tuballighunā bihā aqṣal ghāyāti min jamī'il khayrāti fil ḥayāti wa ba'dal mamāt

Translation: O Allah! Send such blessings upon our Master Muhammad that through them You save us from all terrors and calamities, fulfill all our needs, purify us from all evils, raise us to the highest ranks in Your presence, and help us achieve all our aims of goodness in this life and after death.

3

Durood-e-Taj (The Crown)

For Spiritual Elevation & Divine Blessings

اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی سَیِّدِنَا وَمَوْلَانَا مُحَمَّدٍ صَاحِبِ التَّاجِ وَالْمِعْرَاجِ وَالْبُرَاقِ وَالْعَلَمْ حَامِلِ لِوَآءِ الْحَمْدِ الشَّفِیْعِ الْمُشَفَّعْ یَوْمَ الْمَحْشَرِ الْمَالِکِ لِحَوْضِ الْکَوْثَرْ صَاحِبِ الْمَقَامِ الْمَحْمُوْدِ وَالْحَوْضِ الْمَوْرُوْدِ اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَیْهِ صَلَاۃً تَکُوْنُ لَکَ رِضَاءً وَّلَهٗ جَزَاءً وَّلَنَا شِفَاءً وَعَافِیَۃً

Allāhumma ṣalli 'alā sayyidinā wa mawlānā Muḥammadin ṣāḥibit tāji wal mi'rāji wal burāqi wal 'alam, ḥāmili liwā'il ḥamd, ash-shafī'il mushaffa'i yawmal maḥshar, al-māliki li ḥawḍil kawthar, ṣāḥibil maqāmil maḥmūdi wal ḥawḍil mawrūd. Allāhumma ṣalli 'alayhi ṣalātan takūnu laka riḍā'an wa lahu jazā'an wa lanā shifā'an wa 'āfiyah

Translation: O Allah! Send blessings upon our Master Muhammad, the owner of the crown, ascension, and Buraq, the bearer of the banner of praise, the intercessor whose intercession is accepted on the Day of Judgment, the master of the Pool of Kawthar and the praiseworthy station. O Allah! Send upon him such blessings that become Your pleasure, his reward, and for us healing and wellbeing.

4

Durood-e-Lakhi

Reward of 100,000 Salawat in One Recitation

اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی سَیِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ عَدَدَ مَا فِیْ عِلْمِ اللہِ صَلَاۃً دَآئِمَۃًۢ بِدَوَامِ مُلْکِ اللہِ

Allāhumma ṣalli 'alā sayyidinā Muḥammadin 'adada mā fī 'ilmillāhi ṣalātan dā'imatan bidawāmi mulkillāh

Translation: O Allah! Send blessings upon our Master Muhammad as numerous as what is in Allah's knowledge, blessings that are everlasting with the everlastingness of Allah's kingdom.

✦ Virtue: One recitation equals 100,000 times Durood

5

Durood-e-Nariya

For Urgent Needs & Removal of Difficulties (4444 times)

اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ صَلَاۃً کَامِلَۃً وَّسَلِّمْ سَلَامًا تَامًّا عَلٰی سَیِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدِ نِ الَّذِیْ تَنْحَلُّ بِهِ الْعُقَدُ وَتَنْفَرِجُ بِهِ الْکُرَبُ وَتُقْضٰی بِهِ الْحَوَآئِجُ وَتُنَالُ بِهِ الرَّغَآئِبُ وَحُسْنُ الْخَوَاتِمِ وَیُسْتَسْقَی الْغَمَامُ بِوَجْهِهِ الْکَرِیْمِ وَعَلٰٓی اٰلِهٖ وَصَحْبِهٖ فِیْ کُلِّ لَمْحَۃٍ وَّنَفَسٍۢ بِعَدَدِ کُلِّ مَعْلُوْمٍ لَّکَ

Allāhumma ṣalli ṣalātan kāmilatan wa sallim salāman tāmman 'alā sayyidinā Muḥammadinin-ladhī tanḥallu bihil 'uqad, wa tanfariju bihil kurab, wa tuqḍā bihil ḥawā'ij, wa tunālu bihir raghā'ib, wa ḥusnul khawātim, wa yustasqal ghamāmu biwajhihil karīm, wa 'alā ālihī wa ṣaḥbihī fī kulli lamḥatin wa nafasin bi'adadi kulli ma'lūmin lak

Translation: O Allah! Send complete blessings and perfect peace upon our Master Muhammad through whom difficulties are solved, sorrows are removed, needs are fulfilled, desires are attained, good endings are received, and clouds are sought for rain through his noble countenance. And upon his family and companions in every glance and breath, as many as the number of all things known to You.

✦ Recite 4444 times for urgent needs (or 41 times daily)

6

Durood-e-Akbar (The Greatest)

Most Comprehensive Salutation

اَللّٰهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلٰی سَیِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ فِی الْاَوَّلِیْنَ وَصَلِّ عَلٰی سَیِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ فِی الْاٰخِرِیْنَ وَصَلِّ عَلٰی سَیِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ فِی الْمَلَإِ الْاَعْلٰی اِلٰی یَوْمِ الدِّیْنِ وَصَلِّ عَلٰی سَیِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ وَّعَلٰی جَمِیْعِ الْاَنْبِیَآءِ وَالْمُرْسَلِیْنَ وَعَلٰی اٰلِهِمْ وَاَصْحَابِهِمْ اَجْمَعِیْنَ

Allāhumma ṣalli 'alā sayyidinā Muḥammadin fil-awwalīn, wa ṣalli 'alā sayyidinā Muḥammadin fil-ākhirīn, wa ṣalli 'alā sayyidinā Muḥammadin fil-mala'il a'lā ilā yawmid dīn, wa ṣalli 'alā sayyidinā Muḥammadin wa 'alā jamī'il ambiyā'i wal mursalīn, wa 'alā ālihim wa aṣḥābihim ajma'īn

Translation: O Allah! Send blessings upon our Master Muhammad among the first ones, and send blessings upon our Master Muhammad among the last ones, and send blessings upon our Master Muhammad in the highest assembly until the Day of Judgment, and send blessings upon our Master Muhammad and upon all the Prophets and Messengers, and upon all their families and companions.

7

Durood Sharif (Simple Form)

Quick & Blessed Salutation - Say Anytime

صَلَّی اللہُ عَلَیْہِ وَسَلَّمَ

Ṣallallāhu 'alayhi wa sallam

Translation: May Allah's peace and blessings be upon him.

✦ Recite abundantly - especially on Fridays
